# Vertical Screen Drama Creation Templates

This file provides various creation templates for vertical screen dramas, including character settings, plot structures, and scene templates.

## Creation Type Templates

### 1. Modern Urban Romance

**Theme**: Modern urban romance
**Key Elements**: Meeting, misunderstanding, reconciliation, confession
**Tone**: Warm and romantic

**Protagonist Settings**:
- **Male Lead**:
  - Age: 25-30
  - Occupation: CEO, executive, professional
  - Personality: Cheerful or domineering CEO
  - Traits: Loyal, doting, protective

- **Female Lead**:
  - Age: 22-28
  - Occupation: Workplace newcomer, freelancer
  - Personality: Gentle and kind or independent and strong
  - Traits: Innocent, kind, resilient

**Plot Structure**:
1. **Meeting**: Accidental or arranged first encounter
2. **Getting to Know**: Deepen understanding through events
3. **Misunderstanding**: External obstruction or internal misunderstanding
4. **Separation**: Temporary apart or forced separation
5. **Reunion**: Overcome difficulties to reunite
6. **Confession**: Emotional outburst, establish relationship

### 2. Light Humor Comedy

**Theme**: Light humor comedy
**Key Elements**: Misunderstanding, coincidence, reversal, punchlines
**Tone**: Light and humorous

**Protagonist Settings**:
- **Protagonist**: Ordinary person with distinct personality traits
- **Supporting Characters**: Various funny characters
- **Antagonist**: Ridiculous, not a real threat

**Plot Structure**:
1. **Setup**: Introduce protagonist's interesting characteristics
2. **Conflict**: Protagonist falls into funny predicament
3. **Escalation**: Problems become increasingly complex
4. **Reversal**: Unexpected solution
5. **Resolution**: Happy ending

### 3. Emotional Drama

**Theme**: Emotional drama
**Key Elements**: Conflict, growth, choice, resolution
**Tone**: Profound and moving

**Protagonist Settings**:
- **Protagonist**: Character facing life choices
- **Supporting Characters**: People influencing protagonist's decisions
- **Antagonist**: People hindering protagonist's growth

**Plot Structure**:
1. **Status Quo**: Protagonist's initial state
2. **Inciting Incident**: Event that breaks status quo
3. **Exploration**: Protagonist attempts change
4. **Test**: Face difficult choices
5. **Climax**: Make key decision
6. **Resolution**: Accept outcome and grow

### 4. Suspense Thriller

**Theme**: Suspense thriller
**Key Elements**: Suspense, clues, reversals, truth
**Tone**: Tense and thrilling

**Protagonist Settings**:
- **Protagonist**: Person seeking truth
- **Supporting Characters**: People providing clues or hindrance
- **Antagonist**: Person hiding truth

**Plot Structure**:
1. **Mystery**: Bizarre event occurs
2. **Investigation**: Protagonist begins investigation
3. **Clues**: Discover various clues
4. **Danger**: Protagonist encounters threats
5. **Truth**: Reveal truth
6. **Confrontation**: Face off against true culprit

## Scene Templates

### Scene 1: Identity Reveal Scene

**Purpose**: When protagonist's hidden identity is exposed

**Template**:
```
N. Scene Location Time Interior/Exterior

[Protagonist being humiliated or looked down upon]
[Antagonist arrogant]

[Key character enters]
Key Character: (shocked) You...

Protagonist: (calm response)

[Identity revealed]
Key Character: This is [Identity]!

[Shocked reactions from everyone]
[Antagonist panicked]

Protagonist: (domineering declaration)
```

**Example**:
```
1. High-end Banquet Hall Night Interior
Waitress Lin Qian is clearing a table, being mocked by a group of socialites.

Socialite A: Well, isn't this Lin Qian? Working here now?

Lin Qian: (head down) This is my job.

Socialite B: Heard you were kicked out of the Gu family? How pitiful.

At this moment, banquet hall doors open, a dignified man enters.
Man: (looks around) Tonight's banquet, I am the host.

Socialites: (shocked) Mr... Mr. Gu?

Mr. Gu walks straight to Lin Qian, kneels on one knee.
Mr. Gu: Madam, it's time to come back.

Everyone gasps.
Socialites A and B turn pale.
```

### Scene 2: Reunion Scene

**Purpose**: Reunion of old lovers or acquaintances

**Template**:
```
N. Scene Location Time Interior/Exterior

[Protagonist A doing something]
[Protagonist B appears]

[Two people look at each other]
[Feeling of time reversing]

[Awkward or excited dialogue]
[Emotional surge]
```

**Example**:
```
1. Cafe Day Interior
Su Ya is looking at documents when someone stops at her table.

Su Ya: (looks up) Excuse me...

Gu Yuan: (smiles) Long time no see.

Su Ya freezes, coffee cup falling to the floor.

Gu Yuan: Five years, you haven't changed a bit.

Su Ya: (eyes red) You...

Gu Yuan: I'm here to fulfill my promise from back then.
```

### Scene 3: Crisis Rescue Scene

**Purpose**: Protagonist in danger, gets rescued

**Template**:
```
N. Dangerous Scene Time Interior/Exterior

[Protagonist in danger]
[Antagonist triumphant]

[Rescuer appears]
[Intense confrontation]

[Protagonist rescued]
[Emotional outburst]
```

**Example**:
```
1. Abandoned Warehouse Night Interior
Lin Qian is tied to a chair, several men in black surrounding her.

Man in Black A: Better sign it, don't ask for trouble.

Lin Qian: I won't sign.

Man in Black B: Then don't blame us for being rude.

Man in Black B raises a club, about to strike.

Suddenly, warehouse door is kicked open.

Lu Yuan: (roars) Dare touch my people!

Several bodyguards rush in, fighting the men in black.

Lu Yuan rushes to Lin Qian's side, untying the rope.
Lu Yuan: Are you okay?

Lin Qian: (tears flowing) Why are you here?

Lu Yuan: (gently wipes her tears) I can't lose you again.
```

## Character Relationship Templates

### Relationship 1: Domineering CEO x Ordinary Girl

**Setup**:
- **Male Lead**: Domineering CEO, cold outside warm inside, only gentle to female lead
- **Female Lead**: Ordinary girl, kind and innocent, neither humble nor arrogant
- **Relationship**: Status disparity, true love conquers all

**Plot Points**:
- First meeting: Male lead's special attention to female lead
- Misunderstanding: Female lead thinks male lead is just playing
- Truth: Male lead's deep affection revealed
- Obstacles: Family opposition, ex-girlfriend interference
- Resolution: Overcome difficulties, happy together

### Relationship 2: Hidden Big Shot x Disguised Ordinary

**Setup**:
- **Male Lead**: Hidden identity big shot (CEO, God of War, Miracle Doctor)
- **Female Lead**: Doesn't know male lead's true identity
- **Relationship**: Status gap, eventual identity reveal

**Plot Points**:
- Meeting: Female lead meets "ordinary" male lead
- Interaction: Daily interactions, female lead gradually falls in love
- Crisis: Female lead encounters difficulty, "ordinary" male lead shows ability
- Reveal: Male lead's true identity exposed
- Reversal: Female lead's reaction after knowing truth
- Resolution: Accept true identity, happy together

### Relationship 3: Childhood Sweethearts x Third Party

**Setup**:
- **Male Lead**: Childhood sweethearts with female lead
- **Female Lead**: Torn between two men
- **Second Male Lead**: New suitor
- **Relationship**: Love triangle, ultimate choice

**Plot Points**:
- Status Quo: Female lead and Male 1 are childhood sweethearts
- Change: Male 2 appears, treats female lead well
- Conflict: Female lead's heart begins to waver
- Confrontation: Two men face off
- Choice: Female lead makes final choice
- Resolution: Choose one, other finds peace

## Plot Structure Templates

### Structure 1: Counterattack Revenge (10 episodes)

**Theme**: From suppressed to counterattack revenge

**Episode Outline**:
- Episode 1: Protagonist framed, disgraced
- Episode 2: Protagonist endures humiliation, seeks opportunity
- Episode 3: Protagonist starts plotting, collecting evidence
- Episode 4: Antagonists continue arrogance, audience emotion accumulates
- Episode 5: Protagonist starts counterattacking, small test
- Episode 6: Antagonists panic, audience anticipates explosion
- Episode 7: Protagonist identity revealed, shocks everyone
- Episode 8: Full counterattack, slaps all antagonists
- Episode 9: Truth revealed, antagonists disgraced
- Episode 10: Protagonist at peak, antagonists destitute

**Emotional Curve**: Press-Press-Press-Release-Release

### Structure 2: Sweet Romance (10 episodes)

**Theme**: Domineering CEO dotes on wife

**Episode Outline**:
- Episode 1: Male and female leads meet, male lead pays special attention
- Episode 2: Female lead encounters difficulty, male lead secretly helps
- Episode 3: Increased interaction, ambiguity grows
- Episode 4: Female lead in danger, male lead heroically saves her
- Episode 5: Male lead starts doting on female lead, sweetness escalates
- Episode 6: External obstacles appear, relationship tested
- Episode 7: Misunderstanding deepens, temporarily separate
- Episode 8: Truth revealed, misunderstanding resolved
- Episode 9: Male lead pursues, romance escalates
- Episode 10: Female lead accepts, sweet ending

**Emotional Curve**: Sweet-Angst-Sweet-Sweet

### Structure 3: Suspense Mystery (10 episodes)

**Theme**: Solve case and unravel mystery

**Episode Outline**:
- Episode 1: Bizarre case occurs, police intervene
- Episode 2: Protagonist discovers clues, starts investigation
- Episode 3: Clues complex, trapped in confusion
- Episode 4: Discover important clues, direction clear
- Episode 5: Protagonist encounters danger, suspense escalates
- Episode 6: Breakthrough, true culprit emerges
- Episode 7: True culprit counterattacks, protagonist in trouble
- Episode 8: Desperate counterattack, evidence locked
- Episode 9: Truth revealed, culprit brought to justice
- Episode 10: Case summary, justice served

**Emotional Curve**: Suspense-Doubt-Suspense-Release

## Dialogue Templates

### Dialogue 1: Domineering CEO Style

**Characteristics**: Concise, powerful, unquestioning

```
Male Lead: From today on, she is my woman.
Male Lead: Whoever dares touch a single hair of hers, I'll have their entire family buried with them.
Male Lead: In this world, only I can bully her.
```

### Dialogue 2: Doting Gentle Style

**Characteristics**: Gentle, caring, protective

```
Male Lead: Don't be afraid, I'm here.
Male Lead: I will protect you, not letting you suffer any grievance.
Male Lead: Your wish is my command.
```

### Dialogue 3: Antagonist Arrogant Style

**Characteristics**: Arrogant, contemptuous, looking down on everyone

```
Antagonist: You think you're worthy to compete with me?
Antagonist: Do you think you have the right?
Antagonist: Kneel before me!
```

### Dialogue 4: Identity Reveal Style

**Characteristics**: Shocking, reversal, information-dense

```
Supporting Character: He is...
Male Lead: That's right, I am [Identity].
Everyone: (shocked)
Antagonist: (panicked)
```

## Creation Technique Templates

### Technique 1: Information-First Writing

**Wrong Example**:
```
A: How have you been recently?
B: Okay, I guess. You?
A: I heard you're resigning.
B: Oh, really?
```

**Correct Example**:
```
A: I'm resigning, preparing to start a business.
B: So suddenly? Any plans?
```

### Technique 2: Action Visualization Writing

**Wrong Example**:
```
The male lead is very angry, looking fiercely at the antagonist.
```

**Correct Example**:
```
The male lead grabs the antagonist by the collar and slams him hard against the wall.
Male Lead: (through gritted teeth) Dare say that again?
```

### Technique 3: Suspense Hook Writing

**Ending Suspense Types**:
- Identity suspense: Who is he?
- Truth suspense: What happened?
- Crisis suspense: Can they escape?
- Reversal suspense: What happens next?

**Examples**:
```
[Suspense Example 1 - Identity]
The mysterious person removes their mask, the face revealed shocks everyone.
[Suspense Example 2 - Truth]
The moment the file opens, no one can believe their eyes.
[Suspense Example 3 - Crisis]
Bomb countdown: 3...2...1...
[Suspense Example 4 - Reversal]
Just then, an unexpected person appears.
```
