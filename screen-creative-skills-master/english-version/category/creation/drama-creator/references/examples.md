# Vertical Screen Drama Script Creator - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Creating a Slap Scene Script

### Input

```
Help me create a slap scene where the protagonist is a powerful CEO pretending to be poor to experience life
```

### Output

```
[Analysis: This is a "release spring" scene requiring maximum satisfaction release]

1. High-end Restaurant Day Interior
Female lead Su Ya is dining with friends when her ex-boyfriend enters with his new girlfriend.

Ex-boyfriend: Well, isn't this Su Ya? What, eating here all alone?

Male lead Gu Yan, dressed in a waiter's uniform, comes over to pour water.

Girlfriend: Look at this waiter, can't even pour water properly.

Gu Yan lowers his head to apologize: I'm sorry, I'm sorry.

The ex-boyfriend pushes his luck: That's right, call your manager out!

The restaurant manager rushes in, sees Gu Yan, and immediately bows 90 degrees.
Manager: Mr... Mr. Gu, what are you doing here?

The ex-boyfriend is stunned: What Mr. Gu?

Manager: This gentleman, our group chairman Mr. Gu Yan is personally serving you.

Gu Yan slowly straightens up.
Gu Yan: Manager, please escort these two out. Blacklist them - they are permanently banned from all establishments under the Gu Group.

The ex-boyfriend and girlfriend are humiliated and kicked out of the restaurant.
```

## Example 2: Creating a Suspense Hook

### Input

```
Help me design a suspense hook for a mystery drama that will make audiences want to watch episode 2
```

### Output

```
[Analysis: A suspense hook needs strong conflict + information gap + emotional peak]

1. Abandoned Factory Night Interior
Protagonist Chen Mo is tied to a chair facing a mysterious man in black.

Man in Black: Do you know how your father died?

Chen Mo: ...

The Man in Black takes out a document.
Man in Black: This document can prove everything, but everyone who has seen it hasn't survived past 24 hours.

Chen Mo struggles.
Chen Mo: Give it to me!

The Man in Black sneers.
Man in Black: You want to see it? Fine, I'll show you.

The Man in Black throws the document in front of Chen Mo.

Chen Mo looks down at the document, his eyes widening.

The Man in Black raises a gun, aiming at Chen Mo.
Man in Black: Now, you can go meet your father.

[Just as the gun fires—]

[Screen goes black]
[Gunshot sounds]
[Subtitle: To be continued]

[Analysis: This suspense hook contains multiple hook elements]
1. Mystery of father's death (information hook)
2. Mystery of document contents (content hook)
3. 24-hour death curse (crisis hook)
4. Gunshot at the end (emotional hook)
```

## Example 3: Optimizing an Existing Script

### Input

```
Please help me optimize the following script to better fit vertical screen drama characteristics:

[Original Script]
1. Cafe Day Interior
Xiao Li is drinking coffee when colleague Xiao Zhang walks over.

Xiao Zhang: I heard you're resigning?

Xiao Li: Yes, I want to change environments.

Xiao Zhang: Where are you going?

Xiao Li: Not sure yet, maybe start a business.

Xiao Zhang: Good luck, I support you.

Xiao Li: Thanks.
```

### Output

```
[Problem Analysis]
1. Dialogue is flat, lacking conflict
2. No suspense, no emotional peak
3. Insufficient information, no attraction points

[Optimization Plan - Add "Slap" Elements]

1. Cafe Day Interior
Xiao Li is drinking coffee when colleague Xiao Zhang walks over.

Xiao Zhang: (mocking) Well, I heard you're resigning? Someone like you could actually find a job?

Xiao Li: (calm) Don't worry about me.

Xiao Zhang: Don't tell me you got fired for incompetence? Haha!

At this moment, a luxury car stops at the cafe entrance.

The window rolls down, and a man in a suit waves to Xiao Li.
Man: CEO Li, the meeting is about to begin, please get in.

Xiao Zhang is stunned: CEO... CEO Li?

Xiao Li stands up, adjusting his clothes.
Xiao Li: This is our group's vice president, here to pick me up for a board meeting.

Xiao Li walks out of the cafe and gets into the luxury car.

Xiao Zhang stands there dumbfounded, spraying coffee from his mouth.

[Analysis of Optimizations]
1. Added conflict: Colleague's mockery
2. Added twist: Hidden identity revealed
3. Added satisfaction: Slap in the face to colleague
4. Visual elements: Luxury car, suit, etc.
5. Tight pacing: Information upfront, direct to the core
```

## Example 4: Designing a Story Outline

### Input

```
Help me design a rebirth revenge themed vertical screen drama outline
```

### Output

```
[Theme Analysis]
Rebirth revenge is a popular theme for vertical screen dramas. The core satisfaction points are "foreknowledge" + "rewriting destiny."
Requires designing extreme hatred, extreme revenge, and extreme slaps.

[Story Outline]

Protagonist: Lin Wan (female, betrayed by scumbag and best friend in previous life and killed)
Antagonists: Gu Cheng (scumbag ex-boyfriend), Sophie (green tea best friend)

Episode 1: Death and Rebirth
- Lin Wan discovers Gu Cheng and Sophie's betrayal
- Lin Wan is pushed off a building and dies
- Lin Wan rebirths to three years ago (just got together with Gu Cheng)

Episode 2: Changing Destiny
- Lin Wan decides to change her destiny
- Deliberately approaches Gu Cheng's nemesis - domineering CEO Fu Sihan
- Fu Sihan becomes interested in Lin Wan

Episode 3: Setting Up Revenge
- Lin Wan starts collecting evidence of Gu Cheng and Sophie's crimes
- Pretends to still be Gu Cheng's girlfriend while secretly setting traps
- Fu Sihan becomes Lin Wan's backer

Episode 4: Truth Revealed
- Lin Wan exposes Gu Cheng and Sophie's conspiracy in public
- Fu Sihan appears, supporting Lin Wan
- Gu Cheng and Sophie are disgraced

Episode 5: Ultimate Slap
- Lin Wan and Fu Sihan are together
- Gu Cheng and Sophie are begging on the street (strong contrast)
- Lin Wan and Fu Sihan's luxury car passes by without stopping
- Gu Cheng sees Lin Wan's car, filled with regret

[Emotional Curve]
Press-Press-Release-Press-Release
Episode 1: Press (death)
Episode 2: Press (enduring humiliation)
Episode 3: Press (setting up)
Episode 4: Release (truth revealed)
Episode 5: Release (ultimate slap)

[Suspense Hooks]
Set suspense at the end of each episode:
1. Lin Wan rebirths, audience curious how she'll change destiny
2. Fu Sihan appears, audience curious how he'll help Lin Wan
3. Lin Wan sets traps, audience curious how she'll get revenge
4. Truth revealed, audience curious about antagonists' fate
5. Ultimate slap, audience gets satisfaction release
```
