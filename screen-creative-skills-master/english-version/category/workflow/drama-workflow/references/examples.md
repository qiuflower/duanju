```

During her days in the Cold Palace, Su Wan was disheartened, but by chance, she met a deposed Tai Fei. The Tai Fei was once a favored concubine of the harem who lost everything due to being framed by the Empress. Seeing Su Wan's plight, the Tai Fei felt compassion and decided to teach her palace struggle techniques.

Under the Tai Fei's guidance, Su Wan began to change herself, gradually becoming unfathomable from an innocent maiden. She learned endurance and waiting, learned to protect herself in the complex environment of the harem.

Finally, opportunity came. The Emperor encountered an assassination attempt during a tour, with accompanying guards suffering heavy casualties. Su Wan risked her life to save him, using her own body to block a fatal strike for the Emperor. Her loyalty and bravery moved the Emperor and also let her regain his favor.

After returning to the palace, Su Wan was no longer as innocent as before. She began secretly collecting evidence of the Empress framing her, while actively drawing in other concubines to build her own power. After careful layout, Su Wan publicly exposed the Empress's conspiracy at court. With conclusive evidence, the Empress was sent to the Cold Palace, and Su Wan successfully rose to power, becoming the new Empress.
```
