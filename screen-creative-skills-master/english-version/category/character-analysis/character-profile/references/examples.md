# Character Biography Generation Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Workplace Female Protagonist

### Input

```
Please generate a character biography for the protagonist in the following story:

Lin Qian is a 28-year-old advertising company planning manager who came to Beijing from a small city to make her way. She encountered various challenges at work, but through her own efforts and talent, she gradually established herself in the company. In a bidding project, she met competitor Lu Yuan. The two went from being at odds to appreciating each other, and finally got together.
```

### Output

```
[Character Biography: Lin Qian]

I. Basic Information
- Name: Lin Qian
- Age: 28
- Identity: Advertising Company Planning Manager
- Appearance: Tall and slender, capable temperament, determined eyes

II. Personality Characteristics
- Main Personality: Independent and self-reliant, persevering, outwardly cold but inwardly warm
- Behavioral Characteristics: Decisive in action, pursuing perfection, not easily showing weakness
- Language Characteristics: Concise and comprehensive, hitting the key points, sometimes slightly sharp

III. Background Story
- Family Background: Ordinary family in a small city, parents are teachers
- Growth Experience: Excellent grades since childhood, got into a prestigious university in Beijing through own efforts
- Important Experiences: After graduating from university, ventured to Beijing alone, rose from intern to Planning Manager

IV. Character Relationships
- With Lu Yuan: Developed from competitors to lovers
- With Xiao Li: Colleagues and friends, support each other
- With Manager Zhang: Supervisor-subordinate relationship, also mentor and friend

V. Role in the Story
- Functional Positioning: Core driver of the story
- Plot Driving: Drives plot development through her choices and actions
- Thematic Expression: Showcases the spirit of independent and self-reliant modern women
```

## Example 2: Ancient Court Female Protagonist

### Input

```
Please generate a character biography for the protagonist in the following story:

Su Wan is the legitimate daughter of the Minister of Rites, exceptionally intelligent. She entered the palace for selection and was chosen by the Emperor. After entering the palace, Su Wan established herself through wisdom and caution. The Empress was jealous of Su Wan's favor and plotted to frame her. Su Wan was sent to the Cold Palace. In the Cold Palace, Su Wan met a deposed consort and learned palace intrigue techniques. Finally, Su Wan exposed the Empress's conspiracy and became Empress.
```

### Output

```
[Character Biography: Su Wan]

I. Basic Information
- Name: Su Wan
- Age: 18 (when entering palace) → 22 (when becoming Empress)
- Identity: Legitimate daughter of Minister of Rites → Candidate → Concubine → Empress
- Appearance: Delicate and beautiful features, gentle temperament, intelligent eyes

II. Personality Characteristics
- Main Personality: Exceptionally intelligent, cautious and forbearing, good at learning
- Behavioral Characteristics: Low-key behavior, good at observation, never speaks unnecessarily
- Language Characteristics: Soft voice, appropriate speech, good at listening

III. Background Story
- Family Background: Legitimate daughter of Minister of Rites, received good education since childhood
- Growth Experience: Proficient in music, chess, calligraphy, and painting, exceptionally intelligent, deeply loved by her father
- Important Experiences: Entered palace for selection and was chosen, from being favored to being sent to Cold Palace, to finally becoming Empress

IV. Character Relationships
- With Emperor: Subject-monarch relationship, later became husband and wife
- With Empress: Hostile relationship, from jealousy to framing to counterattack
- With Deposed Consort: Mentor-disciple relationship, the consort taught her palace intrigue techniques

V. Role in the Story
- Functional Positioning: Core protagonist of the story
- Plot Driving: Drives plot development through her growth and transformation
- Thematic Expression: Showcases the growth journey from innocence to complexity
```

## Example 3: Workplace Counterattack Protagonist

### Input

```
Please generate a character biography for the protagonist in the following story:

Su Ya is an ordinary office worker, obscure in the company, often suppressed by her superior. The newly arrived Director Gu Yan pays special attention to Su Ya. In an important project, the superior deliberately made things difficult for Su Ya. Gu Yan secretly helped Su Ya, allowing her to demonstrate her abilities. Su Ya successfully proved herself and gained a promotion. It turns out Gu Yan is Su Ya's university senior, has been silently watching over her. The two confirmed their feelings for each other at work and got together.
```

### Output

```
[Character Biography: Su Ya]

I. Basic Information
- Name: Su Ya
- Age: 26
- Identity: Ordinary office worker → Project Manager
- Appearance: Ordinary appearance but clean and neat, gentle eyes

II. Personality Characteristics
- Main Personality: Introverted and shy, diligent and down-to-earth, not good at words
- Behavioral Characteristics: Works silently, doesn't compete or fight, bears hardships without complaint
- Language Characteristics: Speaks softly, not good at expression, but speaks with substance

III. Background Story
- Family Background: Ordinary working-class family, parents are ordinary employees
- Growth Experience: Introverted personality since childhood, studied hard, average grades
- Important Experiences: After graduating from university, joined an advertising company, has been working obscurely

IV. Character Relationships
- With Gu Yan: Senior-junior relationship, later developed into lovers
- With Superior: Suppression and counter-suppression relationship
- With Colleagues: Friendly but distant colleague relationship

V. Role in the Story
- Functional Positioning: Counterattack protagonist of the story
- Plot Driving: Drives plot development through her growth and counterattack
- Thematic Expression: Showcases the possibility of ordinary people's counterattack
```
