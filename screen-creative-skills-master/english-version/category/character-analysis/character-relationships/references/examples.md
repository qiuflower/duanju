# Character Relationship Analysis Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Urban Romance Story

### Input

```
Please analyze the character relationships in the following story:

Lin Qian and Lu Yuan work at an advertising company and were initially competitors. In the process of collaborating on a project, the two gradually discovered each other's talent and charm, developing from competitors into lovers. However, Lu Yuan's ex-girlfriend suddenly returned to the country, trying to win him back. Faced with his ex-girlfriend's entanglement, Lu Yuan clearly refused and expressed his true feelings to Lin Qian. In the end, the two overcame all obstacles and got together.
```

### Output

```
[Character Relationship Analysis]

I. Relationship Network Overview
- Main Character List: Lin Qian, Lu Yuan, Ex-girlfriend
- Relationship Type Statistics: 1 romantic relationship, 1 competitive relationship (early stage)

II. Main Character Relationship Details

[Relationship 1: Lin Qian ↔ Lu Yuan]
- Relationship Type: Romantic relationship (developed from opponents to lovers)
- Relationship Characteristics: From competition to appreciation, from misunderstanding to understanding, finally achieving success after twists and turns
- Relationship Development Process: Competitors → Appreciation developed during cooperation → Established romantic relationship → Tested by ex's appearance → Final reconciliation
- Reasons for Relationship Change: Work cooperation promoted understanding, ex's appearance triggered testing, confirmation of true feelings promoted stability
- Impact on Story: Core driving force of plot development

[Relationship 2: Lu Yuan ↔ Ex-girlfriend]
- Relationship Type: Past romantic relationship (ended)
- Relationship Characteristics: Ex-girlfriend unilaterally entangles, Lu Yuan clearly refuses
- Relationship Development Process: Former lovers → Breakup → Ex-girlfriend returns to country trying to reconcile → Lu Yuan refuses
- Impact on Story: Acts as test and obstacle, promotes maturity of protagonist relationship

III. Character Relationship Map
- Central Characters: Lin Qian and Lu Yuan
- Secondary Characters: Ex-girlfriend
- Peripheral Characters: None

IV. Character Relationship Change Trajectory
- Initial State: Lin Qian and Lu Yuan are competitors
- Intermediate Changes: From competition to romance to experiencing testing
- Final State: Lin Qian and Lu Yuan finally get together
```

## Example 2: Ancient Court Story

### Input

```
Please analyze the character relationships in the following story:

After entering the palace, Su Wan gained the Emperor's favor. The Empress was jealous of Su Wan and plotted to frame her. Su Wan was sent to the Cold Palace. In the Cold Palace, Su Wan met a deposed consort who taught her palace intrigue techniques. Finally, Su Wan exposed the Empress's conspiracy and became Empress.
```

### Output

```
[Character Relationship Analysis]

I. Relationship Network Overview
- Main Character List: Su Wan, Emperor, Empress, Deposed Consort
- Relationship Type Statistics: 1 romantic relationship, 2 hostile relationships, 1 mentor-disciple relationship

II. Main Character Relationship Details

[Relationship 1: Su Wan ↔ Emperor]
- Relationship Type: Subject-monarch relationship, later developed into husband-wife relationship
- Relationship Characteristics: From favor to neglect to renewed favor
- Relationship Development Process: Gained favor when first entering palace → Lost favor and sent to Cold Palace → Saved Emperor and regained favor
- Impact on Story: Key to Su Wan's fate fluctuations

[Relationship 2: Su Wan ↔ Empress]
- Relationship Type: Hostile relationship
- Relationship Characteristics: Power competition, life-and-death struggle
- Relationship Development Process: Su Wan's favor provoked jealousy → Empress framed Su Wan → Su Wan's successful counterattack
- Impact on Story: Core conflict driving plot development

[Relationship 3: Su Wan ↔ Deposed Consort]
- Relationship Type: Mentor-disciple relationship
- Relationship Characteristics: Passing on experience, guiding direction
- Relationship Development Process: Met in Cold Palace → Consort taught palace intrigue techniques
- Impact on Story: Key turning point in Su Wan's growth

III. Character Relationship Map
- Central Character: Su Wan
- Secondary Characters: Emperor, Empress
- Peripheral Characters: Deposed Consort

IV. Character Relationship Change Trajectory
- Initial State: Su Wan has favor, Empress is jealous
- Intermediate Changes: Su Wan loses favor, grows in Cold Palace
- Final State: Su Wan becomes Empress, Empress is sent to Cold Palace
```
