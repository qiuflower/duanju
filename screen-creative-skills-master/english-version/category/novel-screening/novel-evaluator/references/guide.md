# Novel Evaluation Guide - Story Evaluation Framework

## I. Evaluation Dimensions

Film and television companies generally evaluate story texts from the following dimensions:

### 1. Market Potential

Combined with the market, judge whether the story text can achieve sufficiently good performance in the market.

- **Audience Fit**: Combined with the background and trends of current Chinese film and television market, judge whether the story text sufficiently fits the target audience of this genre type, and whether it can fully attract the target audience.

- **Discussion Heat**: Combined with current Chinese social and cultural background, judge whether the content of the story text fits mainstream social values and can trigger public resonance; whether it is linked to social hot topics, thereby judging whether the entire story can trigger greater discussion heat.

- **Scarcity**: Combined with the background and trends of current Chinese film and television market, analyze whether the story text has sufficient uniqueness and scarcity.

- **Viewership Data**: Combined with the background and trends of current Chinese film and television market, analyze the market prospects of this genre type of story, and judge whether the story text can achieve good viewership data and market performance in the market.

### 2. Innovation Attributes

Judge whether the story text possesses innovation.

- **Core Selection**: Find the most core story selection in the story text, from the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether this story selection is sufficiently fresh and unique, whether it has strong innovation and irreplaceability.

- **Story Concept**: Summarize the most essential story concept of the story text, from the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the story concept of this text is sufficiently prominent and distinctive.

- **Story Design**: From including but not limited to theme, characters, worldview, plot, pacing, narrative, style and other angles, summarize the most outstanding story design of the story text, from the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the story text has sufficiently distinctive characteristics in story design and originality.

### 3. Content Highlights

From the story content level, judge whether the story has strong watchability.

- **Theme**: Combined with current Chinese social and cultural background, analyze whether the theme of the story text is sufficiently clear and definite, whether it can trigger resonance, whether it has sufficient realistic significance and ideological depth.

- **Story Context**: From the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the main story context of the story text has sufficient tension and dramatic nature; whether it is sufficiently attractive to continue following the story.

- **Character Design**: From the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the main character design of the story text is sufficiently novel and characteristic, and impressive.

- **Character Relationships**: From the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the main character relationships of the story text are sufficiently outstanding and distinctive; whether they contain sufficient dramatic tension.

- **Plot Devices**: From the perspective of this genre type of story, judge whether the plot devices in the story text have dramatic tension and watchability; whether their narrative pacing is well-balanced and enticing to follow.

### 4. Overall Evaluation

Combining all dimensions under market potential, innovation attributes, and content highlights, make an overall evaluation and judgment on whether the story is worth further development into film and television drama.

## II. Scoring Standards

Film and television companies generally use scoring to measure the performance of stories in different evaluation dimensions and overall.

- **8.5 points and above**: Indicates excellent, compared to similar story texts, has extremely strong competitiveness and adaptation foundation.
  - If the overall score of the story text reaches 8.5 points and above, it indicates that compared to similar stories, this story text has very strong competitiveness and can directly follow up on subsequent film and television adaptation.

- **Between 8.0-8.4 points**: Indicates good, compared to similar story texts, has relatively strong competitiveness and adaptation foundation.
  - If the overall score of the story text is between 8.0-8.4 points, it indicates that compared to similar stories, this story text has relatively strong competitiveness, but still requires a certain degree of modification to proceed with subsequent film and television adaptation.

- **Between 7.5-7.9 points**: Indicates qualified, compared to similar story texts, no particularly obvious competitiveness, adaptation foundation is average.
  - If the overall score of the story text is between 7.5-7.9 points, it indicates that compared to similar stories, this story text has no obvious competitiveness, performance is average, and requires a relatively large degree of modification to proceed with subsequent film and television adaptation.

- **7.4 points and below**: Indicates poor, compared to similar story texts, almost no competitiveness, adaptation foundation is weak.
  - If the overall score of the story text is 7.4 points and below, it indicates that compared to similar stories, this story text has almost no competitiveness and performance is poor.

## III. Scoring Principles

When scoring specific story texts, please follow the following principles:

- Please strictly and cautiously give scores of 8.0 and above, only a small number of story texts can reach this score.

- When you cannot clearly and definitely judge whether a story belongs to good or excellent, please judge this story in the excellent rating.

- When you cannot clearly and definitely judge whether a story belongs to good or qualified, please judge this story in the qualified rating range.

- If the story text does not have particularly obvious or sufficiently many highlights and competitiveness, even if the plot narration is compact and smooth, and the story is complete, it should only be judged in the qualified rating range. A large number of similar story texts with similar competitiveness are concentrated in the range between 7.5-7.9 points.

- Scores must be refined to one decimal place.

## IV. Evaluation Process

1. **In-depth Reading**: Use relevant skills to deeply read the story text provided by the user, and form your own understanding of the story.

2. **Multi-dimensional Analysis**: According to evaluation dimensions, scoring standards, and scoring principles, strictly and carefully analyze and score each dimension of the story text.

3. **Overall Evaluation**: Form an overall evaluation and score, and give the user advice on whether to continue developing or how to develop this story.

## V. Notes

- Always maintain a strict attitude toward scoring story texts. Analysis and scoring under strict and detailed requirements are very important to users.

- Strictly judge and score the text based on the content presented by the story text.

- When evaluating story texts, pay attention to distinguishing the difference between writing style and story content, focus on evaluating the story content presented by the text, and do not place the focus of evaluation on whether the writing style is outstanding.
