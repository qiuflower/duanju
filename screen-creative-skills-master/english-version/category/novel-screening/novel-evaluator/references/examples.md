## Example 1: Evaluate Urban Romance Novel

### Input

```
Please evaluate and score the following novel:

The story of Lin Qian and Lu Yuan developing from competitors to lovers at an advertising company. Lin Qian is a 28-year-old planning manager, independent and strong. Lu Yuan is the creative director, brilliant and talented. The two first met during a bidding competition and got to know each other during cooperation. The appearance of an ex-girlfriend created tests, but they ultimately overcame obstacles and got together.
```

### Output

```
[Novel Evaluation Report]

[Market Potential]: Score: 7.8
- Audience Fit: Highly aligned with target audience for urban emotional genre, especially suitable for women aged 25-35
- Discussion Heat: Genre close to life, easy to trigger audience resonance with first love and reunion
- Scarcity: Reunion genre is relatively common, innovation is average
- Viewership Data: Meets current market demand for urban emotional dramas, expected performance is good

[Innovation Attributes]: Score: 7.5
- Core Selection: Workplace romance, but many similar genres exist
- Story Concept: Emotions are sincere, but story concept is relatively traditional
- Story Design: Narrative structure is relatively complete, character growth line is clear

[Content Highlights]: Score: 7.8
- Theme: Explores love and growth, has certain depth
- Story Context: Delicate emotions, but dramatic tension is average
- Character Design: Three-dimensional character images, but insufficient innovation
- Character Relationships: Emotional relationship portrayal is delicate, infectious
- Plot Devices: Plot development is relatively flat, lacking explosion points

[Overall Evaluation]: Score: 7.7
The overall quality of this IP is above average, emotions are sincere and delicate, character relationship portrayal is in place, but innovation and dramatic nature are relatively insufficient. Compared to similar urban emotional IPs, competitiveness is above average.

[Follow-up Recommendation]: Recommend proceeding, but requires the following modifications:
1. Enhance dramatic tension and conflict intensity of plot
2. Add more innovative elements to character design
3. Optimize pacing, add memorable points and explosion points
```
