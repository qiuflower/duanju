# Story Outline Generator Expert (Novel Version) - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Urban Emotional Novel

### Input

```
Please generate a 500-800 word story outline for the following novel:

Lin Qian is a 28-year-old advertising company planning manager who came to Beijing from a small city to struggle. In a bidding project, she met competitor Lu Yuan. The two were confrontational during the bidding process, but gradually discovered each other's talent and charm during cooperation. After the project succeeded, they started dating. However, Lu Yuan's ex-girlfriend suddenly returned from abroad, trying to win him back. Facing the ex-girlfriend's entanglement and tests from family background differences, they finally overcame all obstacles and got together.
```

### Output

```
Protagonist Lin Qian is a 28-year-old advertising company planning manager who came to Beijing alone from a small city to struggle. In this strange city, she stood firm in the company through her own effort and talent. In an important project bidding, Lin Qian met competitor Lu Yuan. Lu Yuan is the company's creative director, brilliant and talented but introverted. The two were confrontational during the bidding process, each refusing to yield, both wanting to prove their proposal was superior. However, the company's senior management decided the two must cooperate to complete this important project. In the forced cooperation process, Lin Qian and Lu Yuan gradually discovered each other's strengths and charm. Lin Qian appreciated Lu Yuan's talent and creativity, while Lu Yuan was attracted by Lin Qian's resilience and independence. As the project deepened, the competition between the two gradually transformed into appreciation and tacit understanding. After the project succeeded, the company held a celebration dinner. At the dinner, Lu Yuan proactively invited Lin Qian to dance, and the two talked happily in the dance floor, further drawing closer their distance. From that night on, Lin Qian and Lu Yuan began officially dating. However, their relationship was not smooth sailing. Lu Yuan's ex-girlfriend suddenly returned from abroad, trying to salvage this relationship. The ex-girlfriend's intervention created cracks in Lin Qian and Lu Yuan's relationship, and they developed misunderstandings and estrangement. At the same time, Lin Qian's parents learned she was dating a rich second-generation and worried she would be hurt, also expressing opposition. Facing the entanglement of the ex-girlfriend and the pressure from family background differences, Lin Qian and Lu Yuan both fell into pain and confusion. After a period of separation and reflection, Lin Qian and Lu Yuan both realized each other's importance. Lu Yuan clearly refused the ex-girlfriend, indicating to her that he had let go of the past. Lin Qian also had deep communication with her parents, letting them see Lu Yuan's sincerity and determination. Finally, Lin Qian and Lu Yuan overcame all obstacles and got back together. Their relationship had been tested and became more mature and stable. With the blessings of friends and family, the two started a new life together.
```

## Example 2: Costume Palace Intrigue Novel

### Input

```
Please generate a 500-800 word story outline for the following novel:

Su Wan is the legitimate daughter of the Minister of Rites, exceptionally intelligent. She entered the palace for selection and was chosen by the Emperor. After entering the palace, Su Wan discovered the harem was full of intrigue. Su Wan stood firm in the harem through her wisdom and caution. The Empress was jealous of Su Wan's favor and designed a frame-up. Su Wan was sent to the Cold Palace. In the Cold Palace, Su Wan met a deposed Tai Fei, and the Tai Fei taught her palace struggle techniques. The Emperor encountered an assassination attempt during a tour, and Su Wan risked her life to save him, regaining favor. Su Wan designed to expose the Empress's conspiracy and finally became the Empress.
```

### Output

```
Su Wan is the legitimate daughter of the Minister of Rites, exceptionally intelligent since childhood, with clear and beautiful features. In the year of the palace selection, Su Wan was chosen to enter the palace. When first entering the palace, she was not adapted to this complex environment, but through her intelligent mind and cautious personality, she gradually stood firm in the harem. The Emperor's favor made Su Wan the focus of the harem, but also attracted the jealousy of the Empress and other concubines. The Empress was the Emperor's wife during his residence before ascending the throne, with noble status, but being childless for many years made her feel threatened. She viewed Su Wan as a thorn in her side and designed to frame Su Wan for losing composure before the Emperor. Su Wan was sent to the Cold Palace, going from a favored concubine to a forgotten废人. In the days of the Cold Palace, Su Wan was disheartened, but by chance, she met a deposed Tai Fei. The Tai Fei was once also a favored concubine of the harem, losing everything due to being framed by the Empress. The Tai Fei saw Su Wan's plight and felt compassion, deciding to teach her palace struggle techniques. Under the Tai Fei's guidance, Su Wan began to change herself, gradually becoming unfathomable from an innocent maiden. She learned endurance and waiting, learned to protect herself in the complex environment of the harem. Finally, opportunity came. The Emperor encountered an assassination attempt during a tour, with accompanying guards suffering heavy casualties. Su Wan risked her life to save him, using her own body to block a fatal strike for the Emperor. Her loyalty and bravery moved the Emperor and also let her regain the Emperor's favor. After returning to the palace, Su Wan was no longer as innocent as before. She began secretly collecting evidence of the Empress framing her, while actively drawing in other concubines to build her own power. After careful layout, Su Wan publicly exposed the Empress's conspiracy at court. With conclusive evidence, the Empress was sent to the Cold Palace, and Su Wan successfully rose to power, becoming the new Empress. Standing at the peak of power, Su Wan looked back on the past, filled with emotion in her heart. She went from an innocent maiden to an Empress skilled in power schemes, and along this journey, there was both her own effort and too many people's sacrifices. She decided to be a good Empress, using her power to bring justice and order to the harem.
```

## Example 3: Workplace Inspirational Novel

### Input

```
Please generate a 500-800 word story outline for the following novel:

Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker, obscure in the company, often suppressed by her supervisor. New director Gu Yan pays special attention to Su Ya. In an important project, the supervisor deliberately made things difficult for Su Ya. Gu Yan secretly helped Su Ya, letting her demonstrate her abilities. Su Ya successfully proved herself and obtained a promotion. It turned out that Gu Yan was Su Ya's college senior, always silently following her. The two confirmed their feelings at work and got together.
```

### Output

```
Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker who has worked at an advertising company for three years but has always been obscure. Her supervisor is a snobbish villain who often deliberately makes things difficult for and suppresses Su Ya, keeping her in a neglected position in the company. The arrival of new director Gu Yan brought new atmosphere to the company. Gu Yan is young and promising, a well-known creative genius in the industry. To Su Ya's surprise, this new director pays special attention to her, often giving her guidance and help in her work. In an important project, the supervisor intentionally excluded Su Ya from the core team to let his own protégé shine. Gu Yan saw this but did not directly intervene, instead secretly creating opportunities for Su Ya to demonstrate her abilities. At a client meeting, Su Ya seized the opportunity and presented a wonderful creative proposal, winning high appreciation from the client. This success let the company's senior management see Su Ya's talent, and she finally received due recognition and opportunity. In subsequent work, through her own effort and Gu Yan's support, Su Ya gradually stood firm in the company and finally obtained a promotion, going from ordinary white-collar worker to project supervisor. At a company gathering, Gu Yan revealed a secret to Su Ya: it turned out he was Su Ya's senior from college, always silently following her growth. In college, Gu Yan was impressed by this hard-studying junior sister but didn't have the courage to express his feelings. After graduation, Gu Yan had been looking for opportunities to approach Su Ya until this transfer to her company. Su Ya was moved upon learning the truth. She recalled the bits and pieces of getting along with Gu Yan and finally understood his special care for her. The two confirmed their feelings at work and got together. Su Ya's counterattack from obscure to workplace success not only realized her career dream but also harvested sincere love. This story tells us that as long as we persist unremittingly and struggle hard, we will definitely welcome our own spring.
```
