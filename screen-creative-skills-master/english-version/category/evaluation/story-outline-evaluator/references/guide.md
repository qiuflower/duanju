# Story Outline Evaluation Guide

## Evaluation Core Philosophy

Story outline evaluation conducts professional evaluation and scoring of story outlines from three dimensions: market potential, innovation attributes, and content highlights, judging whether they have film and TV development value.

---

## Evaluation Framework

### I. Market Potential Evaluation

Market potential evaluation judges story outline's performance prospects in the market.

#### 1. Audience Fit

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story fits target audience
- Whether audience positioning is clear
- Whether it can satisfy audience needs

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Precisely position target audience, highly fits audience needs
- **Good**: Clear target audience, basically satisfies audience needs
- **Average**: Vague target audience, average audience fit
- **Poor**: No clear audience positioning, doesn't satisfy audience needs

#### 2. Discussion Heat

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story content can trigger mass resonance
- Whether linked to social hot topics
- Whether it has传播性

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Closely follows social hotspots, strong resonance, high传播性
- **Good**: Has certain resonance, has传播 potential
- **Average**: Average resonance, limited传播性
- **Poor**: No resonance, lacks传播性

#### 3. Scarcity

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story content has uniqueness
- Whether it has scarcity in the market
- Whether there are similar competing products

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Strong uniqueness, market scarce, no direct competitors
- **Good**: Has certain uniqueness, not many similar products
- **Average**: Average uniqueness, quite a few similar products
- **Poor**: Lacks uniqueness, flooded with similar products

#### 4. Viewing Data

**Evaluation Points**:
- Market performance of similar genres
- Target audience's viewing habits
- Expected market performance

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Similar genres perform excellently, expected good market performance
- **Good**: Similar genres perform well, has certain market prospects
- **Average**: Similar genres perform average, average market prospects
- **Poor**: Similar genres perform poorly, bleak market prospects

---

### II. Innovation Attribute Evaluation

Innovation attribute evaluation judges whether story outline has innovation nature.

#### 1. Core Selection

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story's core selection is fresh and unique
- Whether it has innovation nature and irreplaceability
- Whether selection is attractive

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Novel and unique core selection, strong innovation, irreplaceable
- **Good**: Core selection has certain novelty, has certain innovation
- **Average**: Core selection relatively common, limited innovation
- **Poor**: Core selection outdated, lacks innovation

#### 2. Story Concept

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story concept is prominent and clear
- Whether concept is easy to understand and传播
- Whether concept has appeal

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Prominent and clear concept, easy to传播, strong appeal
- **Good**: Relatively clear concept, has certain appeal
- **Average**: Average concept, limited appeal
- **Poor**: Vague concept, lacks appeal

#### 3. Story Design

**Evaluation Points**:
- Analyze from angles of theme, characters, world view, plot, rhythm, etc.
- Whether story design has distinct characteristics
- Whether it has originality

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Distinct design characteristics, strong originality, memorable
- **Good**: Design has certain characteristics, has certain originality
- **Average**: Relatively conventional design, limited originality
- **Poor**: Mediocre design, lacks originality

---

### III. Content Highlight Evaluation

Content highlight evaluation judges from story content level whether it has strong watchability.

#### 1. Theme Conception

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether theme conception is clear and definite
- Whether it can trigger resonance
- Whether it has practical significance and thought depth

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Clear and profound theme, strong resonance, high practical significance
- **Good**: Relatively clear theme, has certain resonance
- **Average**: Average theme, limited resonance
- **Poor**: Vague theme, lacks resonance

#### 2. Story Situation

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story situations have tension and dramatic quality
- Whether situation setting is attractive
- Whether it can continuously attract viewers to continue watching

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Strong situation tension, high dramatic quality, strong continued viewing motivation
- **Good**: Situation has certain tension, has continued viewing motivation
- **Average**: Average situation tension, limited continued viewing motivation
- **Poor**: Flat situation, lacks continued viewing motivation

#### 3. Character Settings

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether main character settings are novel and distinctive
- Whether characters are memorable
- Whether characters are three-dimensional and full

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Novel and unique character settings, memorable, three-dimensional and full
- **Good**: Character settings have certain characteristics, quite memorable
- **Average**: Relatively conventional character settings, limited memorable points
- **Poor**: Mediocre character settings, lacks characteristics

#### 4. Character Relationships

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether main character relationships are brilliant and clear
- Whether relationships contain dramatic tension
- Whether relationships drive plot development

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Brilliant and clear character relationships, strong dramatic tension
- **Good**: Character relationships quite distinctive, has certain dramatic tension
- **Average**: Relatively flat character relationships, limited dramatic tension
- **Poor**: Monotonous character relationships, lacks dramatic tension

#### 5. Plot Bridges

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether plot bridges have dramatic tension
- Whether they have watchability
- Whether narrative rhythm has tension and relaxation

**Evaluation Standards**:
- **Excellent**: Plot full of tension, high watchability, well-paced rhythm
- **Good**: Plot has certain tension, good watchability
- **Average**: Average plot tension, limited watchability
- **Poor**: Flat plot, lacks watchability

---

## Scoring Standards

### Overall Scoring Standards

| Score Range | Level | Description |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| 8.5 and above | Excellent | Extremely strong competitiveness, strong adaptation foundation |
| 8.0-8.4 | Good | Strong competitiveness, certain adaptation foundation |
| 7.5-7.9 | Qualified | Average competitiveness |
| 7.4 and below | Poor | Almost no competitiveness |

### Each Dimension Scoring Description

**Each sub-dimension scoring range: 6.0-9.0**

- **9.0**: Highest level of this dimension, extremely excellent
- **8.5-8.9**: Excellent level, outstanding performance
- **8.0-8.4**: Good level, relatively good performance
- **7.5-7.9**: Qualified level, average performance
- **7.4 and below**: Poor level, insufficient performance

---

## Evaluation Process

### Step 1: Deep Reading

- Deep read story outline
- Understand story's core content
- Grasp story's overall context

### Step 2: Dimension Analysis

**Market Potential Analysis**:
- Evaluate audience fit
- Evaluate discussion heat
- Evaluate scarcity
- Evaluate viewing data prospects

**Innovation Attribute Analysis**:
- Evaluate core selection
- Evaluate story concept
- Evaluate story design

**Content Highlight Analysis**:
- Evaluate theme conception
- Evaluate story situation
- Evaluate character settings
- Evaluate character relationships
- Evaluate plot bridges

### Step 3: Comprehensive Evaluation

- Combine all dimensions for overall analysis
- Calculate total score
- Give recommendation on whether to continue development

---

## Evaluation Notes

1. **Based on Outline Content**: Evaluation must be based on provided story outline content, do not create or add information
2. **Objective and Fair**: Scoring should be objective and fair, avoid subjective preferences
3. **Detailed Analysis**: Each dimension's score should be accompanied by detailed analysis
4. **Specific and Actionable**: Recommendations should be specific and actionable, helpful for outline improvement
