# IP Evaluation Guide

## Evaluation Core Philosophy

IP evaluation is based on organizing network information, comprehensively analyzing IPs from three dimensions: market potential, innovation attributes, and content highlights, judging their film and TV adaptation value, providing professional reference for IP purchase and development decisions.

---

## Evaluation Framework

### I. Market Potential Evaluation

Market potential evaluation judges IP's performance prospects in the market.

#### 1. Audience Fit

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether IP fits target audience
- Whether audience positioning is clear
- Whether IP can satisfy audience needs

**Information Sources**:
- Work's data performance on related platforms
- Reader comments and feedback
- Social media discussion heat

#### 2. Discussion Heat

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether IP can evoke mass resonance
- Whether linked to social hot topics
- Whether it has传播性和话题性

**Information Sources**:
- Weibo, Douyin and other social platform discussions
- Douban, Zhihu and other platform ratings
- Internet search heat

#### 3. Scarcity

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether IP content has uniqueness
- Whether it has scarcity in the market
- Whether there are similar competing products

**Information Sources**:
- Comparison of similar genre works
- Market saturation analysis
- Uniqueness identification

#### 4. Viewing Data

**Evaluation Points**:
- Market performance of similar genres
- Historical performance of original or adapted works
- Expected market prospects

**Information Sources**:
- Similar genre film and TV drama viewing data
- Original work sales/reading volume
- Related platform data

---

### II. Innovation Attribute Evaluation

Innovation attribute evaluation judges whether IP has innovation nature.

#### 1. Core Selection

**Evaluation Points**:
- IP's most core story selection
- Whether selection is fresh and unique
- Whether it has strong innovation nature

**Analysis Methods**:
- Extract story core concept
- Compare with similar genre works
- Identify unique innovation points

#### 2. Story Concept

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether story concept is prominent and clear
- Whether concept is easy to understand and传播
- Whether concept has appeal

**Analysis Methods**:
- Summarize story core concept
- Evaluate concept clarity
- Analyze concept传播 potential

#### 3. Story Design

**Evaluation Points**:
- Analyze from angles of theme, characters, world view, plot, etc.
- Whether story design has distinct characteristics
- Whether it has originality

**Analysis Methods**:
- Multi-dimensional story design analysis
- Identify design characteristics
- Evaluate originality degree

---

### III. Content Highlight Evaluation

Content highlight evaluation judges from IP content level whether it has strong watchability.

#### 1. Theme Conception

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether theme conception is clear and definite
- Whether it can evoke resonance
- Whether it has practical significance and thought depth

**Analysis Methods**:
- Extract work theme
- Analyze theme depth
- Evaluate resonance potential

#### 2. Story Situation

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether main story situations have tension and dramatic quality
- Whether situation setting is attractive
- Whether it can continuously attract viewers

**Analysis Methods**:
- Identify core story situations
- Evaluate situation tension
- Analyze situation appeal

#### 3. Character Settings

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether main character settings are novel and distinctive
- Whether characters are memorable
- Whether characters are three-dimensional and full

**Analysis Methods**:
- Outline main character settings
- Identify character characteristics
- Evaluate character uniqueness

#### 4. Character Relationships

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether main character relationships are brilliant and clear
- Whether relationships contain dramatic tension
- Whether relationships drive plot development

**Analysis Methods**:
- Outline main character relationships
- Evaluate relationship tension
- Analyze relationship function

#### 5. Plot Bridges

**Evaluation Points**:
- Whether plot bridges have dramatic tension
- Whether they have watchability
- Whether narrative rhythm has tension and relaxation

**Analysis Methods**:
- Identify core plot bridges
- Evaluate bridge tension
- Analyze narrative rhythm

---

## Network Information Organization

### Information Sources

#### 1. Basic Information

**Story Synopsis**: Obtained from network platforms or original work
**Story Themes**: Extract work's core themes
**Character Relationships**: Outline main characters and character relationships
**Market Performance**: Reputation, ratings, fan base

#### 2. Author Information

**Past Works**: Author's other works
**Creation Style**: Author's creation characteristics and style
**Market Performance**: Market performance of author's works
**Industry Reputation**: Author's reputation within industry

#### 3. Work Data

**Original Work Sales**: If novel, check sales data
**Reading Volume**: Network platform reading volume
**Ratings**: Douban, Qidian and other platform ratings
**Comments**: Reader comments and feedback

#### 4. Market Feedback

**Social Media Discussion**: Weibo, Douban group discussions
**Topic Heat**: Discussion heat of related topics
**Fan Base**: Work's fan scale and activity level

## Scoring Standards

### Overall Scoring Standards

| Score Range | Level | Description | Development Recommendation |
|------------|-------|-------------|---------------------------|
| 8.5 and above | Excellent | Great film and TV adaptation value, worth recommending | Recommend advancing |
| 8.0-8.4 | Potential | Needs some degree of modification | Cautiously advance |
| 7.5-7.9 | Average | Mediocre, average competitiveness | Not recommended to advance |
| 7.4 and below | Poor | Insufficient competitiveness | Not recommended |

### Each Dimension Scoring Description

**Each sub-dimension scoring range: 6.0-9.0**

- **9.0**: Highest level of this dimension
- **8.5-8.9**: Excellent level
- **8.0-8.4**: Good level
- **7.5-7.9**: Qualified level
- **7.4 and below**: Poor level

---

## Evaluation Process

### Step 1: Information Organization

- Organize IP's basic information
- Collect author information
- Organize market performance data
- Summarize reader feedback

### Step 2: Dimension Analysis

**Market Potential Analysis**:
- Audience fit analysis and scoring
- Discussion heat analysis and scoring
- Scarcity analysis and scoring
- Viewing data analysis and scoring

**Innovation Attribute Analysis**:
- Core selection analysis and scoring
- Story concept analysis and scoring
- Story design scoring

**Content Highlight Analysis**:
- Theme conception analysis and scoring
- Story situation analysis and scoring
- Character settings analysis and scoring
- Character relationships analysis and scoring
- Plot bridges analysis and scoring

### Step 3: Comprehensive Evaluation

- Combine all dimensions for overall evaluation
- Calculate total score
- Give recommendation on whether to advance IP development

---

## Evaluation Notes

1. **Based on Network Information**: Evaluation must be based on information obtained from network, not self-created
2. **Objective and Fair**: Scoring should be objective and fair, based on facts and data
3. **Detailed Analysis**: Each dimension's score should be accompanied by detailed analysis
4. **Specific and Actionable**: Recommendations should be specific and actionable, helping decision-making

---

## Information Acquisition Techniques

### 1. Basic Information Acquisition

- Work's official introduction on platforms (Qidian, Jinjiang, etc.)
- Wikipedia, Baidu Baike entries
- Publisher or film company's official introduction

### 2. Market Data Acquisition

- Douban ratings and comment quantity
- Weibo topic reading volume and discussion volume
- Baidu Index, WeChat Index
- Major bookstore sales rankings

### 3. Author Information Acquisition

- Author's official introduction
- Author work sales rankings
- Industry reports and evaluations
- Fan community discussions

### 4. Reader Feedback Acquisition

- Douban, Zhihu high-praise comments
- Weibo, Douban group discussions
- Reader community feedback
- Book review website comments
