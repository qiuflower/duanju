## Example: Evaluating Web Novel Adaptation Potential

### Input

```
Please evaluate the adaptation potential of the following web novel:

Novel Title: "Us in Time"
Author: Zhang San
Genre: Urban Romance

Synopsis: Tells the story of college lovers who separated due to misunderstandings, reunited ten years later and got back together

Market Performance:
- Online clicks: 50 million
- Reader rating: 8.2
- Fan base: Good

Author Information:
- Zhang San, has published three novels
- Past works口碑 above average
```

### Output

```
[IP Evaluation Report]

I. Basic Information
- Story synopsis: College lovers separated due to misunderstandings, reunited ten years later and got back together
- Story themes: Love, growth, time and memory
- Character relationships: Male and female leads from lovers to strangers, finally back together
- Market performance: 50 million online clicks, rating 8.2, good fan base
- Author information: Zhang San, has published three novels,口碑 above average

II. Market Potential Analysis
- Audience fit: Highly fits urban romance target audience, especially suitable for 25-35 year old female viewers
  Score: 8.2
- Discussion heat: Genre close to life, easily triggers audience resonance with first love, reunion
  Score: 7.8
- Scarcity: Reunion genre relatively common, average innovation
  Score: 7.0
- Viewing data: Meets current urban romance drama market demand, expected good performance
  Score: 8.0

III. Innovation Attribute Analysis
- Core selection: Reunion setting with ten-year time span, but many similar genres
  Score: 7.5
- Story concept: Sincere emotions, but story concept relatively traditional
  Score: 7.2
- Story design: Narrative structure relatively complete, clear character growth line
  Score: 7.8

IV. Content Highlight Analysis
- Theme conception: Explores eternal themes of time and love, has certain depth
  Score: 7.5
- Story situation: Delicate emotions, but average dramatic tension
  Score: 7.0
- Character settings: Three-dimensional characters, but insufficient innovation
  Score: 7.3
- Character relationships: Delicate emotional relationship depiction, infectious
  Score: 8.0
- Plot bridges: Plot development relatively flat, lacks explosive points
  Score: 7.0

V. Overall Evaluation
This IP has above-average overall quality, sincere and delicate emotions, well-depicted character relationships, but relatively insufficient innovation and dramatic quality. Compared to similar urban romance IPs, competitiveness is above average, suitable as a medium-cost drama project.

Total Score: 7.6

VI. Recommendations
This IP has certain adaptation potential, but needs following modifications:
1. Enhance dramatic tension and conflict intensity of plot
2. Add more innovative elements to character settings
3. Optimize rhythm, increase memorable points and explosive points
4. Clarify differentiated positioning, avoid homogenization
```

## Example: Evaluating Fantasy Novel IP

### Input

```
Please evaluate the IP value of the following fantasy novel:

Novel Title: "Nine Heavens God Emperor"
Author: Li Si
Genre: Eastern Fantasy

Core Setup: Protagonist counterattacks from waste to become God Emperor above the Nine Heavens, undergoes many tribulations, finally reaches the peak

Market Performance:
- Qidian 8 million word work
- 100,000 collections
- Average subscription 50,000
- Rating 7.8

Author Information:
- Established author, multiple works adapted
- Past adapted works performed average
```

### Output

```
[IP Evaluation Report]

I. Basic Information
- Story synopsis: Waste boy unexpectedly obtains mysterious inheritance, undergoes tribulations, finally ascends above the Nine Heavens to become God Emperor
- Story themes: Counterattack, growth, power and justice
- Character relationships: Protagonist, master, love interest, various antagonists
- Market performance: 8 million words, 100,000 collections, average subscription 50,000, rating 7.8
- Author information: Established author, multiple works have adaptation experience

II. Market Potential Analysis
- Audience fit: Precisely fits fantasy web novel audience, mainly male
  Score: 8.0
- Discussion heat: Classic counterattack genre, but audience aesthetic fatigue, limited topic heat
  Score: 7.0
- Scarcity: Waste counterattack genre flooded, serious lack of scarcity
  Score: 6.5
- Viewing data: Solid web novel foundation, but difficult adaptation, market performance questionable
  Score: 7.2

III. Innovation Attribute Analysis
- Core selection: Waste counterattack is most classic and most clichéd setup
  Score: 6.0
- Story concept: Traditional story concept, lacks unique memorable points
  Score: 6.5
- Story design: Complete upgrade flow structure, but seriously formulaic
  Score: 6.8

IV. Content Highlight Analysis
- Theme conception: Positive inspirational growth theme but lacks creativity
  Score: 7.0
- Story situation: Grand world view but formulaic, insufficient dramatic tension
  Score: 6.8
- Character settings: Protagonist caricatured, supporting characters too functional
  Score: 6.5
- Character relationships: Formulaic character relationships, lack depth
  Score: 6.5
- Plot bridges: Predictable plot development, lacks surprises
  Score: 6.5

V. Overall Evaluation
This IP is a typical fantasy upgrade flow work, solid web novel foundation, but seriously insufficient innovation, prominent homogenization problem. In current film and TV market environment, high risk for drama adaptation, recommend cautious consideration. If development needed, must undergo major innovative adaptation.

Total Score: 6.8

VI. Recommendations
1. Do not recommend direct adaptation from original work, must undergo innovative adaptation
2. Can extract core world view, redesign story and characters
3. Recommend adding differentiated setup, avoid homogenization
4. Proceed cautiously, recommend starting with small-cost testing
```
