# Major Plot Point Analysis Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Three-Act Structure - Urban Romance Drama

### Input

```
Lin Qian is a 28-year-old advertising company planning manager, came to Beijing from small city to struggle. In bidding for an important project, she meets competitor Lu Yuan.

The two are confrontational during competition, but gradually discover each other's talent and charm during cooperation, developing from competitors to lovers.

However, Lu Yuan's ex-girlfriend suddenly returns, trying to win him back. Facing ex-girlfriend's entanglement and test of family background differences, they finally overcome all obstacles and get together.
```

### Output

```
[Stage 1: First Meeting and Competition (Establishment)]
- Lin Qian and Lu Yuan meet for first time in project bidding, confrontational
- Two become competitors, compete in multiple projects
- During competition, both demonstrate respective professional abilities and personality traits

[Stage 2: Cooperation and Mutual Understanding (Development)]
- Important project requires two to cooperate, from confrontation to adaptation
- Discover each other's talent and charm during work cooperation, relationship begins to transform
- Two gradually understand each other, from competitors to partners to friends
- Affection gradually buds, but neither has clearly expressed yet

[Stage 3: Test and Fulfillment (Climax and Resolution)]
- After project success, two start dating, relationship officially established
- Ex-girlfriend returns, creates emotional obstacles and conflicts
- Workplace competition and family background differences bring new tests
- Lu Yuan clearly refuses ex-girlfriend, expresses sincerity to Lin Qian
- Two overcome all obstacles, finally get together
```

## Example 2: Five-Act Structure - Costume Palace Intrigue Drama

### Input

```
Su Wan is the legitimate daughter of Minister of Rites, exceptionally intelligent. She enters palace for selection, chosen by Emperor. After entering palace, she discovers harem is full of intrigue, Empress and concubines fight openly and secretly.

Su Wan gradually stands firm in harem through her wisdom and caution. However, Empress is jealous of Su Wan's favor, designs framing her. Su Wan is sent to Cold Palace.

In Cold Palace, Su Wan meets a deposed Tai Fei, Tai Fei teaches her palace struggle techniques. Su Wan gradually understands that to survive in harem, must become strong.

Finally, opportunity comes. Emperor encounters assassination during tour, Su Wan risks life to save him, regains Emperor's favor. After returning to palace, Su Wan starts counterattack, designs exposing Empress's conspiracy, finally becoming new Empress.
```

### Output

```
[Stage 1: Entering Palace (Setup)]
- Su Wan enters palace for selection as Minister of Rites' legitimate daughter
- Chosen by Emperor, first enters harem
- Discovers complex environment and open/secret fighting in harem

[Stage 2: Gaining Favor (Development)]
- Su Wan gains Emperor's favor through wisdom and caution
- Gradually stands firm in harem
- Attracts Empress's jealousy and hostility

[Stage 3: Being Framed (Conflict)]
- Empress designs framing Su Wan
- Su Wan sent to Cold Palace, encounters major setback
- From favored to unfavored, fate falls to bottom

[Stage 4: Growth (Turning Point)]
- Meets deposed Tai Fei in Cold Palace
- Learns palace struggle techniques, mindset undergoes transformation
- From innocent to scheming, gradually grows

[Stage 5: Counterattack (Climax and Resolution)]
- Emperor encounters assassination, Su Wan risks life to save him
- Regains favor, fate shows turning point
- Designs exposing Empress's conspiracy, successful counterattack
- Finally becomes new Empress
```

## Example 3: Vertical Screen Drama Structure - Urban Counterattack Drama

### Input

```
Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker, obscure in company, often suppressed by supervisor. One day, a new director Gu Yan joins company, paying special attention to Su Ya.

In important projects, Su Ya is deliberately obstructed by supervisor. Gu Yan secretly helps her, allowing her to demonstrate her abilities. Finally, Su Ya successfully proves herself, gets promoted.

It turns out Gu Yan is Su Ya's senior from college, silently following her all along. Two gradually develop feelings at work, finally get together.
```

### Output

```
[Stage 1: Suppression (Episodes 1-3)]
- Su Ya obstructed and suppressed by supervisor at workplace
- Obscure, looked down upon by colleagues
- New director Gu Yan joins company, pays special attention to Su Ya

[Stage 2: Turning Point (Episodes 4-6)]
- In important project, supervisor deliberately obstructs Su Ya
- Gu Yan secretly helps Su Ya
- Su Ya demonstrates ability, gains recognition
- Supervisor's obstruction fails, but doesn't give up

[Stage 3: Climax (Episodes 7-8)]
- Su Ya shines in key project
- Supervisor's conspiracy exposed
- Su Ya gets promoted, successful counterattack
- Gu Yan's identity revealed, is Su Ya's senior

[Stage 4: Fulfillment (Episodes 9-10)]
- Gu Yan expresses feelings to Su Ya
- Two confirm relationship, get together
- Supervisor fired, evil punished
```

## Example 4: Multi-line Narrative Structure - Suspense Mystery Drama

### Input

```
Criminal investigation team captain Chen Mo takes over bizarre serial murder case. Killer always leaves a poker card at crime scene, each card's number represents victim's death order.

Chen Mo's investigation discovers all victims related to an accident ten years ago. That accident was case his father was responsible for, father died unexpectedly during investigation.

Meanwhile, forensic doctor Xiao Lin discovers new clues on victims, pointing to mysterious organization. Chen Mo begins to suspect killer might be survivor of accident ten years ago.

Finally, Chen Mo finds true killer is actually his most trusted partner. Partner's family died in accident ten years ago, he潜伏警队多年，just for revenge. In final confrontation, Chen Mo personally arrests his partner.
```

### Output

```
[Stage 1: Case Occurs (Mainline Opens)]
- Serial murder case occurs, poker card signature left at scene
- Chen Mo takes over case investigation
- Discovers all victims related to accident ten years ago

[Stage 2: Deep Investigation (Multiple Lines Parallel)]
- Chen Mo line: Discovers father previously responsible for that accident investigation
- Forensic Dr. Xiao Lin line: Discovers new clues, pointing to mysterious organization
- Partner line: Secretly assists investigation, but behavior suspicious

[Stage 3: Truth Surfaces (Clues Converge)]
- Chen Mo discovers truth about father's death
- Forensic Dr. Xiao Lin confirms mysterious organization exists
- Chen Mo begins to suspect people around him

[Stage 4: Shocking Reversal (Climax)]
- Chen Mo discovers true killer is partner
- Partner confesses motive: family died in accident
- Two engage in intense confrontation

[Stage 5: Justice Served (Resolution)]
- Chen Mo personally arrests partner
- Case truth revealed
- Chen Mo completes father's unfinished work
```

## Example 5: Rebirth Revenge Structure - Rebirth Drama

### Input

```
Lin Wan killed by husband Gu Cheng and best friend Su Fei. She rebirths to three years ago, when she just got together with Gu Cheng.

Lin Wan decides to change fate, she starts collecting evidence of Gu Cheng and Su Fei's crimes. Meanwhile, she deliberately approaches Gu Cheng's nemesis - domineering CEO Fu Sihan.

In public, Lin Wan exposes Gu Cheng and Su Fei's conspiracy. Fu Sihan appears to support her, Gu Cheng and Su Fei disgraced. Finally, Lin Wan and Fu Sihan get together, achieve happiness.
```

### Output

```
[Stage 1: Rebirth (Premise Setting)]
- Lin Wan killed by Gu Cheng and Su Fei
- Rebirths to three years ago
- Decides to change fate, starts revenge plan

[Stage 2: Layout (Planning Stage)]
- Lin Wan maintains relationship with Gu Cheng on surface
- Secretly collects evidence of Gu Cheng and Su Fei's crimes
- Deliberately approaches Fu Sihan, establishes new relationship

[Stage 3: Development (Relationship Advancement)]
- Lin Wan and Fu Sihan relationship gradually becomes closer
- Continues collecting evidence, waiting for opportunity
- Gu Cheng and Su Fei still unaware

[Stage 4: Counterattack (Climax Erupts)]
- Exposes conspiracy in public
- Fu Sihan appears to support Lin Wan
- Gu Cheng and Su Fei disgraced
- Revenge successful

[Stage 5: New Life (Happy Ending)]
- Lin Wan and Fu Sihan confirm relationship
- Start new life
- Gu Cheng and Su Fei receive legal punishment
```

## Plot Point Extraction Techniques

### 1. Stage Division Principles

**Three-Act Structure** (applicable to most stories):
- Act 1: Establishment (introduce characters, set goals, establish conflicts)
- Act 2: Development (conflict escalation, character growth, plot advancement)
- Act 3: Climax and Resolution (final confrontation, resolve conflicts, achieve goals)

**Five-Act Structure** (applicable to complex stories):
- Act 1: Setup (introduce background, establish character relationships)
- Act 2: Development (conflicts appear, plot advancement)
- Act 3: Conflict (crisis appears, contradictions intensify)
- Act 4: Turning Point (opportunity appears, situation reverses)
- Act 5: Climax and Resolution (final resolution, new balance)

**Vertical Screen Drama Structure** (applicable to short dramas):
- Suppression Stage (create conflicts, accumulate emotions)
- Turning Point Stage (opportunity appears, release emotions)
- Climax Stage (conflict erupts, emotional peak)
- Fulfillment Stage (problem resolved, goals achieved)

### 2. Plot Point Expression Points

- **Concise and Accurate**: Each plot point expression no more than 150 words
- **Objective and Faithful**: Strictly summarize according to original meaning, do not create
- **Logically Coherent**: Ensure logical coherence between plot points, foreshadowing and呼应
- **Clear Priority**: Highlight main plots, downplay secondary plots
- **Clear Stages**: Each stage has clear plot main theme

### 3. Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Miss important plot turning points
- ❌ Unreasonable stage division, doesn't fit story structure
- ❌ Plot point expression too long or too brief
- ❌ Arbitrarily add plots not in original text
- ❌ Lack logical coherence between stages
