# Story Outliner Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Urban Workplace Story

### Input

```
Please generate an outline for the following story:

After graduating from university, I came to Beijing and started a new life in this strange city. At the beginning, I didn't understand anything at work and was severely criticized by my supervisor Manager Zhang. My colleague Xiao Li took pity on me and taught me a lot. Slowly, I adapted to the work and gained Manager Zhang's recognition. After work, I liked to eat hotpot and chat with my neighbor Xiao Wang. One day, I met Xiaomei, a girl I had a crush on in college, on the street. She was also working in Beijing. We reconnected and talked a lot about our college memories. But Xiaomei already had a boyfriend and was preparing to get married. Although I was a bit disappointed, I still wished her well. After this incident, I decided to work even harder and take root in Beijing.
```

### Output

```
After graduating from university, the protagonist came to Beijing alone and began a new life in this strange city. When first arriving in Beijing, the protagonist felt unfamiliar and uneasy about the work environment, often being criticized by the strict supervisor Manager Zhang for being unfamiliar with the business. Fortunately, colleague Xiao Li saw the protagonist's difficulties and proactively provided help and guidance. With Xiao Li's assistance, the protagonist gradually adapted to the work rhythm, significantly improved their professional abilities, and finally gained Manager Zhang's recognition. In life, the protagonist became good friends with neighbor Xiao Wang, and they often ate hotpot and chatted together after work, sharing their lives. By chance, the protagonist met Xiaomei, a girl they had a crush on in college, on the street, discovering she was also working in Beijing. The two reconnected and reminisced about the good times in college together. However, the protagonist learned that Xiaomei already had a boyfriend and was about to get married. Although somewhat disappointed in their heart, the protagonist still sincerely wished her well. After this emotional setback, the protagonist became even more determined to work hard in Beijing and take root for development, full of confidence in the future.
```

## Example 5: Workplace Counterattack Story

### Input

```
Please generate an outline for the following story:

Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker, obscure in the company, often suppressed by her supervisor. The new director Gu Yan pays special attention to Su Ya. In an important project, the supervisor deliberately made things difficult for Su Ya. Gu Yan secretly helped Su Ya, letting her demonstrate her abilities. Su Ya successfully proved herself and obtained a promotion. It turned out that Gu Yan was Su Ya's senior from college, who had been silently following her. The two confirmed their feelings at work and got together.
```

### Output

```
Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker, working obscurely in the company, often subjected to deliberate suppression and unfair treatment by her supervisor. After new director Gu Yan joined the company, he paid special attention to Su Ya, which puzzled her. In an important project, the supervisor deliberately made things difficult for Su Ya, trying to embarrass her. At the critical moment, Gu Yan secretly helped Su Ya, giving her the opportunity to demonstrate her true abilities. Su Ya performed excellently in the project, successfully proved her worth, and gained recognition and promotion from the company's senior management. Gu Yan's identity was finally revealed - he turned out to be Su Ya's senior from college, who had been silently following her growth. The two gradually understood each other at work, confirmed their feelings, and finally got together.
```
