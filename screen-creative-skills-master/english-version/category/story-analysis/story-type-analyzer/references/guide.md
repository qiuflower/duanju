# Genre Type and Creative Extraction Guide

## I. Basic Definitions

### Story Type

"Story Type" is a dramatic concept that refers to the way or method of describing and classifying different types of stories. It involves categorizing and explaining story elements, plot structure, themes, style, etc., in order to better understand and analyze stories.

Generally analyze stories by **theme** and **plot structure**:
- **Theme**: Including romance, adventure, science fiction, horror, comedy, suspense, action, history, etc.
- **Plot Structure**: For example, traditional three-act structure (setup, development, climax) or five-act structure (introduction, rising action, turning point, climax, resolution).

## II. Analysis Dimensions

### 1. Genre and Theme

Analyze the story's main narrative genre (such as science fiction, suspense, romance, etc.) and core theme (such as growth, revenge, redemption, etc.).

### 2. Plot Structure

Analyze the story's overall architecture, identifying key plot turning points and structural patterns (such as Hero's Journey, Save the Cat, etc.).

### 3. Core Creative

Extract the most innovative and unique settings or concepts in the story, which is the key to distinguishing it from similar works.

### 4. Story Highlights

Summarize the engaging elements in the story, such as unique character settings, unexpected reversals, profound emotional expressions, etc.

## III. Workflow

1. **Reading Comprehension**: Fully read and understand the provided story text, organize contextual relationships, resolve repetition or breaks in expression.

2. **Genre Positioning**: Based on the definition of story genre, summarize the main story genre and narrative theme of the story.

3. **Structure Analysis**: Analyze and summarize the basic plot structure of the story.

4. **Creative Extraction**: Summarize and extract the core creative of the story.

5. **Highlight Summary**: Summarize and extract the main highlights of the story.

## IV. Output Standards

Analysis results should include the following parts:

- **[Genre and Theme]**: Summarize the genre and narrative theme of the story text.

- **[Plot Structure]**: Summarize and extract the main plot structure of the story text.

- **[Core Creative]**: Summarize and extract the core creative of the story text.

- **[Story Highlights]**: Summarize and extract the main highlights of the story text.

## V. Best Practices

- **Accuracy**: Strictly summarize according to the content expressed in the original story text, do not create yourself.
