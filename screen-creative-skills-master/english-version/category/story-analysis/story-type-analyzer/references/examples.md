# Genre Type and Creative Extraction Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Fantasy Transmigration Story

### Input

```
Analyze the genre type of the following story:

Modern youth Li Ming, while visiting a museum, unintentionally touched an ancient artifact and suddenly transmigrated to a martial arts world. He discovered he brought modern scientific knowledge with him and began using physical principles to explain martial arts moves. He also brought modern business models to ancient times, creating a brand new business empire. During adventures, Li Ming made friends with heroes from all walks, finally helping the martial arts world quell a catastrophe.
```

### Output

```
[Genre Type Analysis]
- Dominant Genre: Fantasy Transmigration
- Auxiliary Genres: Martial Arts, Adventure, Growth, Business Struggle
- Genre Fusion Degree: High

[Creative Element Extraction]
1. Core Creative: Collision and fusion of modern knowledge system with ancient martial arts world
2. Innovation Point 1: Using modern physical principles to explain and improve martial arts moves, bringing brand new understanding of martial arts
3. Innovation Point 2: Innovative application of modern business models in ancient jianghu
4. Innovation Point 3: Artifact transmigration setting, connecting ancient and modern worlds

[Story Features]
- Setting Feature: Fresh perspective and interest from collision of ancient and modern cultures
- Narrative Feature: Adopting dual perspective of ancient-modern contrast, enhancing immersion

[Genre Value]
- Commercial Value: High, matches current young audience's preference for fresh genres
- Artistic Value: Medium, unique creative concept but limited depth
```

