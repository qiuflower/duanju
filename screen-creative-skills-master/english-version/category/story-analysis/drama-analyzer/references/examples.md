# Script Analysis Expert - Detailed Examples

## Example 1: Suspense Novel Plot Analysis

### Input

```
Protagonist Li Ming is a senior journalist. One day he receives a mysterious letter claiming to know the truth about his father's accidental death twenty years ago. The letter provides an address and time, requiring him to go alone.

Li Ming goes to the designated location and finds it's an abandoned warehouse. In the warehouse, he finds a diary whose owner is his father's colleague before his death. The diary records a shocking secret: Father's death was not an accident, but murder.

Just as Li Ming prepares to leave, he suddenly encounters unidentified persons tracking him. He quickly drives away, but on the way home, discovers his car has been tampered with, brakes fail.
```

### Output

```
[Plot Point]: Protagonist receives mysterious letter claiming to know truth about father's death
[Dramatic Function]: Set suspense hook, trigger audience curiosity, drive protagonist to investigate, establish story's core driving force

[Plot Point]: Protagonist finds diary in abandoned warehouse, discovers truth about father's death
[Dramatic Function]: Reveal core secret, advance plot development, deepen suspense, foreshadow subsequent conflicts

[Plot Point]: Protagonist encounters unidentified persons tracking, car tampered with
[Dramatic Function]: Create tense atmosphere, escalate conflict, imply secret organization behind, increase urgency and danger
```

## Example 2: Romance Story Plot Analysis

### Input

```
Lin Qian is a 28-year-old advertising company planning manager, independent and strong. In an important project bidding, she meets competitor Lu Yuan. The two are confrontational during bidding, but gradually discover each other's talent and charm during cooperation.

After project success, they start dating. However, Lu Yuan's ex-girlfriend suddenly returns, trying to win him back. Facing ex-girlfriend's entanglement, Lu Yuan clearly refuses and expresses sincerity to Lin Qian. Finally, they overcome all obstacles and get together.
```

### Output

```
[Plot Point]: Lin Qian and Lu Yuan meet in project bidding, confrontational
[Dramatic Function]: Establish initial conflict, showcase character personalities, foreshadow subsequent relationship development

[Plot Point]: Two gradually understand each other during cooperation, affection buds
[Dramatic Function]: Resolve initial conflict, advance relationship development, showcase character charm, enhance audience anticipation

[Plot Point]: Ex-girlfriend returns, trying to win back Lu Yuan, create emotional obstacle
[Dramatic Function]: Set emotional conflict, test protagonist feelings, increase dramatic tension, drive plot climax

[Plot Point]: Lu Yuan clearly refuses ex-girlfriend, expresses sincerity to Lin Qian
[Dramatic Function]: Resolve emotional conflict, confirm protagonist feelings, achieve emotional sublimation, satisfy audience expectations
```

## Example 3: Workplace Counterattack Story Analysis

### Input

```
Su Ya is an ordinary workplace white-collar worker, obscure in the company. One day, a new director joins the company, paying special attention to Su Ya.

In important projects, Su Ya is suppressed by her supervisor. The new director secretly helps her, allowing her to demonstrate her abilities. Finally, Su Ya successfully proves herself and gets promoted.

It turns out the new director is Su Ya's senior from college, silently following her all along.
```

### Output

```
[Plot Point]: New director joins company, pays special attention to Su Ya
[Dramatic Function]: Set suspense hook, imply relationship, trigger audience curiosity

[Plot Point]: Su Ya suppressed by supervisor at work
[Dramatic Function]: Create conflict and suppression, accumulate audience emotions, build power for subsequent reversal

[Plot Point]: New director secretly helps Su Ya
[Dramatic Function]: Showcase character relationship, advance plot development, imply true identity

[Plot Point]: Su Ya demonstrates ability, successfully promoted
[Dramatic Function]: Achieve emotional release, satisfy audience expectations, complete character growth

[Plot Point]: New director identity revealed, is Su Ya's senior
[Dramatic Function]: Create emotional surprise, deepen character relationship, give audience warm feeling
```

## Example 4: Costume Palace Intrigue Story Analysis

### Input

```
Su Wan is the legitimate daughter of the Minister of Rites, exceptionally intelligent. She enters palace for selection, chosen by the Emperor.

Finally, Emperor encounters assassination during tour, Su Wan risks life to save him, regaining favor. After returning to palace, Su Wan designs exposing Empress's conspiracy, finally becoming new Empress.
```

```
[Plot Point]: Su Wan enters palace for selection, chosen by Emperor
[Dramatic Function]: Establish story starting point, showcase protagonist identity, foreshadow subsequent conflicts

[Plot Point]: Empress designs framing, Su Wan sent to Cold Palace
[Dramatic Function]: Create major setback, escalate conflict, drive protagonist growth

[Plot Point]: Su Wan meets Tai Fei in Cold Palace, learns palace struggle techniques
[Dramatic Function]: Showcase protagonist growth, accumulate abilities, prepare for subsequent counterattack

[Plot Point]: Su Wan risks life to save Emperor, regains favor
[Dramatic Function]: Achieve fate turning point, release audience emotions, drive plot climax

[Plot Point]: Su Wan exposes Empress's conspiracy, becomes new Empress
[Dramatic Function]: Complete revenge arc, achieve ultimate goal, satisfy audience expectations
```

## Example 5: Rebirth Revenge Story Analysis

### Input

```
Lin Wan is killed by husband Gu Cheng and best friend Su Fei. She rebirths to three years ago, when she just got together with Gu Cheng.

Lin Wan decides to change fate, she starts collecting evidence of Gu Cheng and Su Fei's crimes. Meanwhile, she deliberately approaches Gu Cheng's nemesis - domineering CEO Fu Sihan.

In public, Lin Wan exposes Gu Cheng and Su Fei's conspiracy. Fu Sihan appears to support her, Gu Cheng and Su Fei disgraced. Finally, Lin Wan and Fu Sihan get together, achieving happiness.
```

### Output

```
[Plot Point]: Lin Wan victimized, rebirths to three years ago
[Dramatic Function]: Set core suspense, establish story premise, provide motivation for revenge

[Plot Point]: Lin Wan decides to change fate, starts collecting criminal evidence
[Dramatic Function]: Clarify protagonist goal, advance plot development, establish revenge tone

[Plot Point]: Lin Wan approaches Gu Cheng's nemesis Fu Sihan
[Dramatic Function]: Introduce new character, create new relationship thread, foreshadow subsequent reversal

[Plot Point]: Lin Wan exposes Gu Cheng and Su Fei's conspiracy
[Dramatic Function]: Achieve revenge climax, release audience emotions, complete main conflict

[Plot Point]: Lin Wan and Fu Sihan get together, achieve happiness
[Dramatic Function]: Achieve emotional satisfaction, complete story loop, give audience happy ending
```
